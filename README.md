# Financial Performance Dashboard

A comprehensive interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze global sales, profit, and product performance metrics across multiple segments and countries.

##  Live Dashboard Preview
![Financial Dashboard](https://github.com)

## Project Overview
This project transforms raw transactional data into actionable financial intelligence. It enables executives and stakeholders to track key performance indicators (KPIs), identify top-performing regions, and understand product-level profitability.

## Key Insights Derived
Based on the dashboard metrics, here are the core business takeaways:

### 1. High-Level Performance
* **Total Net Sales:** \$118.73M
* **Total Units Sold:** 1.13M units
* **Total Profit:** \$16.89M

### 2. Seasonality & Sales Trends
* **Q4 Surge:** Sales spike dramatically in October, hitting the yearly peak (~\$21M+), followed by a sharp correction in November and a strong recovery in December.
* **Mid-Year Stability:** Sales remain relatively stable between January and August, fluctuating safely between \$5M and \$10M monthly.

### 3. Product & Segment Winners
* **Top Product:** **Paseo** is the clear market leader, driving **\$33.0M** in net sales. **Carretera** is the lowest performing at \$13.8M.
* **Core Segment:** **Government** contracts dominate the business, accounting for **44.22%** of total net sales, closely followed by **Small Business** at **35.74%**. Midmarket represents the smallest footprint at 2.01%.

### 4. Geographical Profitability
* **Top Market:** **France** leads global profitability with **\$3.8M** in profit.
* **Balanced Distribution:** Profit is distributed relatively evenly across all key regions, with Mexico trailing slightly behind the leader at \$2.9M.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Features
* **Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Modeling:** Star schema with custom Date and Product dimensions.
* **DAX Measures:** Dynamic KPIs for Net Sales, Units Sold, and Profit margins.
* **Interactivity:** Dynamic slicers for **Year (2013-2014)** and **Discount Bands (High/Low)**.
